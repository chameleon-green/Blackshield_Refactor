Outwardly, power weapons are identical to primitive melee implements, their true nature only
betrayed by the hazy halo of energy surrounding their blades. Thanks to this molecular 
disruption field, they are capable of slicing or crushing through virtually any form of armor.

The Proteus-pattern power sword is a fine weapon, with a heavy adamantium blade and a rugged
field generator. It is bulky and brutal, sacrificing a degree of speed for killing power.
